Nonviolent Persons and Practices

The Nonviolence Persons and Practices website began as a substitute to Professor Kirk Trombley's final research paper for his Justice Studies 701 Course of the same name. Taught at the University of New Hampshire in Durham, the course serves to inform senior level Justice Studies majors of the various persons and practices throughout history and the modern day that have utilized nonviolence in their movements. The content on the website was written by the graduating class of 2018 and provided the first round of content on the website. Our goal is to build a resource that an be used as a research hub both in the United States and around the world.
